What is AutoClient?

AutoClient is an AI-driven solution designed to streamline your marketing efforts by scraping the web for potential leads, saving their information in your CRM, and sending highly personalized emails to ensure the highest engagement.

Getting Started Guide

  • Use the navigation menu to explore features and tools.
  • Start a new lead search to find potential customers.
  • View and manage your saved leads in the CRM section.
  • Create and monitor email campaigns for effective outreach.
  • Check analytics to measure your success and improve strategies.

Latest Updates

  • New feature: Real-time lead scoring and prioritization!
  • Integration with the latest CRM software for seamless data management.
  • Enhanced email templates for even better engagement rates.

AI-Powered Lead Generation

AutoClient uses advanced AI to find potential customers based on your search criteria.

  • Enter a search term to find potential leads in your industry.
  • Define the number of results you want to retrieve.
  • Email addresses and phone numbers will be saved to the CRM automatically.
  • AI-customized emails will be sent automatically to maximize engagement.
